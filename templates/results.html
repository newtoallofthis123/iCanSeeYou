Why is the Creeper's Message Important

The creeper in this case is rather friendly right? That won't always be the case. In real life, the creeper can be anything, a website, a blog, a video, a picture, a friend, a real creeper or anyone. It is very hard to protect your self from this. I am only learning web development and if I could do this, trust me, anyone could. It is not very hard. I used 2 free api's, api's, which are free, freegeoip and timeapi. They are obviously great tools which have many non nefarious use cases but, well, they can be used for this. Getting this data was not very hard. All it took was some time and effort and I had all I need. This is not to scare you and make you paranoid about the websites you visit, but to just show you what can be done

What You Can Do

You cannot do much to protect yourself from such tracking, because, well, you can't. The only way to protect yourself a little, is to well:

  • Not open any suspicious links in your email, although a lot worse could happen then.
  • Try to know which websites you visit, do a simple virustotal check, see if it is https, all the little stuff like that matters
  • As for the big tech tracking, you can't do much. Just hope for the best.
  • Try to clear out your cache and unneccessary cookies either on exit or whenever you can.
  • Disable Javascript wherever possible, a lot more can be done using it as it is well suited for interaction with the browser

Want a better and informative scare? visit coveryourtracks, a free tool from the EFF.
